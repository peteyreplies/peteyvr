About Villa Leonardo

Located in Cape Coral, Florida, Villa Leonardo has been painstakingly prepared to have you relaxed and in vacation mode as soon as you walk in the door. Lovely landscaping, complete with palm trees, welcomes you to a spacious, modern home furnished with everything you need for a great family vacation.

Featuring:

  • Four bedrooms which sleep 10 people comfortably
  • Private, heated 14'x 26' foot pool with lovely lanai enclosed by screen dome to keep the bugs out
  • Two boat lifts in a backyard saltwater canal with direct access to the Gulf of Mexico
  • Well-equipped kitchen, living room, dining room, and laundry room
  • Free WiFi, 6 HDTVs, Weber grill, multi-zone wireless sound system and board games galore

Our family has rented homes all over the world and felt like it was time to be the host instead. We know what a great vacation home should feel like and try to make that experience a reality for each and every one of our guests.

Stay with us!

Beatiful backyard.

Just behind our house you'll find a well-maintained lawn sloping gently down to a boat dock along Cape Coral's famous network of canals. With two boat lifts on a canal mere minutes from the Gulf, Villa Leonardo is a perfect place to rent or bring a boat.

Lovely lanai.

You'll love our lanai. Modeled after the famous roofed patios of Hawaii, you can enjoy the outdoors protected from the elements, getting just as much sun as you want a few feet from our private, heated pool.

Bountiful bedrooms.

With four bedrooms, six beds, and a pullout couch to boot, everyone in your party will have all the comfort and privacy they need to rest after fun in the sun
